This page is a snapshot of what I am focused on now, both in work and outside it.

## Work

I am focused on backend and platform engineering, especially reliable systems, AI-connected workflows, and the engineering habits that make complex software easier to operate, debug, and reason about.

I am also continuing to refine the Obsidian-to-Hugo publishing flow behind this site.

## Writing and Tools

- Obsidian for private notes and long-term knowledge work.
- Hugo and GitHub Pages for publishing.
- Codex and AI-assisted workflows for engineering experiments.
- Shottr for screenshots and lightweight visual annotation.

## Outside Work

I play table tennis two or three times a week. It is one of the few activities where I can feel improvement through repetition, timing, and tiny corrections.

I also like cycling, cooking, and getting outside on the water. I hold a Singapore kayaking 2-star certification.

## Listening

Two songs I keep coming back to because they carry a kind of vitality I like:

- [浜崎あゆみ - MY ALL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ntpxLWnf7h4&list=RDntpxLWnf7h4&start_radio=1)
- [中島美嘉 - 僕が死のうと思ったのは](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnLDHIDOT00&list=RDlnLDHIDOT00&start_radio=1)

They do not deny the hard parts of life: failure, aging, illness, loss, and the ordinary weight of reality. But they still move. That feeling matters to me: seeing clearly, not pretending, and still choosing to go forward today.
